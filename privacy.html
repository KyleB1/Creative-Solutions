Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Creative Web Solutions ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, protects, and discloses your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Contact us through our website or email
  • Sign up for our newsletter
  • Create a customer or support portal account
  • Request a quote or schedule a consultation
  • Use our services or pay for a project

Information collected includes: name, email, phone number, company name, project details, payment information (processed securely), and any content you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and process requests
  • To send billing and transactional emails
  • To send newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data in transit uses HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Payment Security: Payment processing via Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant)
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access with password protection
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security reviews regularly
  • No Storage: We do not store credit card numbers on our servers

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, payment processing, email, analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Your consent (e.g., API integrations you authorize)

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve features and user experience. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out extension.

6. Data Retention

  • Project data: Retained for 7 years (for legal/financial compliance)
  • Support tickets: Retained for 3 years
  • Newsletter subscribers: Retained until unsubscribed
  • You can request data deletion anytime (subject to legal holds)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise these rights: Email support@creativeweb.solutions with your request.

8. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, we comply with GDPR. We process data only with your lawful consent or legitimate business interest. You have all rights outlined in GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@creativeweb.solutions
  • Or use the contact form on our website

Security Practices

🔒 Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS). At-rest data is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms.

💳 Payment Security

We use Stripe for all payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified, the highest security standard for payment processing. We never store full credit card numbers on our servers.

🔐 Access Controls

Employee access to customer data is restricted to those who need it to perform their job. All access is logged and monitored. We use multi-factor authentication for admin accounts.

🛡️ Hosting & Infrastructure

We host on major cloud providers (AWS/Google Cloud) with industry-standard security measures including firewalls, intrusion detection, and DDoS protection.

🔄 Regular Audits

We conduct quarterly security reviews and penetration testing to identify and remediate vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

📋 Incident Response

If a security breach occurs, we will notify affected customers within 24 hours and provide guidance on steps to take. We maintain incident response procedures and insurance.

Portal Security (Customer & Support Accounts)

If you use our customer portal or support portal, the following additional protections apply:

  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt (never stored in plain text)
  • Session Security: Sessions timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Optional 2FA available for enhanced security
  • Rate Limiting: Login attempts are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Activity Logging: All account activity is logged for security monitoring

Third-Party Links & Services

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Always review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting the updated policy here. Your continued use of our services implies acceptance of our updated privacy policy.
