Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know below.

About Our Services

▼ What exactly do you build?
We specialize in three main areas: (1) Conversion-focused landing pages and websites designed to turn visitors into leads, (2) Secure client portals with ticketing systems and project tracking, and (3) Operational automation tools that reduce manual work.
▼ Can you integrate with my existing tools?
Yes. We work with Stripe, Zapier, Mailchimp, Salesforce, and most popular business tools. If your tool has an API, we can likely integrate it. Custom integrations may cost extra.
▼ Do you handle hosting and domain setup?
We recommend hosting providers and can assist with DNS configuration. You can use your existing host or we can help you choose one. Hosting costs are separate from our fees.

Timeline & Turnaround

▼ How long does a typical project take?
Starter projects: 3-4 weeks. Professional: 5-8 weeks. Enterprise: 8-12 weeks or longer. Timeline depends on scope, your feedback speed, and design complexity. Rush options available (+20% fee).
▼ Can I see progress during development?
Absolutely. We provide weekly updates, demo links, and review sessions. You'll have a dedicated Slack channel or email thread for real-time communication.
▼ What's your revision policy?
Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds (typically 2-3). Beyond that, we charge $75/hour. We're committed to getting it right, so major feedback is usually accommodated included in scope.

Support & Maintenance

▼ What happens after launch?
Your pricing includes support for a set period (1-3 months depending on tier). After that, you can purchase ongoing support plans starting at $500/month for monitoring and updates.
▼ Do you provide training?
Yes. We include a brief training session on site basics. For more complex features, we offer extended training at $125/hour or include it in your support plan.
▼ What if I find a bug after launch?
Critical bugs (breaks functionality) are fixed immediately. Minor bugs are prioritized based on your support plan. Emergency fixes outside support hours are billed at $200/hour.

How We Work

▼ What's the first step?
Schedule a free 30-minute consultation. We'll discuss your goals, timeline, and budget. No pressure—we're primarily here to understand your needs and see if we're a good fit.
▼ Do you need specific information from me to start?
Yes! We'll ask for your brand guidelines, content, target audience, competitors' websites for inspiration, and any existing assets (logos, photos, copy). The more you provide upfront, the faster we move.
▼ Do I own the final website?
Completely. You own all code, content, and design. We provide you with full source files and can transfer hosting to you at any time.
▼ Can you redesign an existing site?
Yes. Redesign projects often start at $3,500-$8,000 depending on complexity. We can migrate your content or start fresh based on your preference.
